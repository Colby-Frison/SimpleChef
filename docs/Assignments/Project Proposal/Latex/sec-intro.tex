SimpleChef is a new mobile-first cooking assistant application designed to reduce friction across the entire cooking process---from discovering a recipe to meal planning and grocery shopping. The tagline ``Your calm kitchen companion'' captures the app's goal: to serve as a capable, calm kitchen companion that streamlines daily cooking tasks.

Existing cooking applications often fragment the experience. Users may browse recipes in one app, track calories in another, and maintain grocery lists in notes or paper. Recipe instructions are frequently buried in long blocks of text, making it difficult to follow steps while cooking. Timer management during cooking can be cumbersome when users must switch between apps or rely on separate kitchen timers. SimpleChef addresses these pain points by unifying recipe discovery, step-by-step cooking mode with integrated timers, meal planning with calorie tracking, and an auto-generating grocery list into a single, cohesive interface.The application targets home cooks who want to cook more often but find the logistics---planning meals, tracking ingredients, following instructions---overwhelming. By applying Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) principles such as progressive disclosure, clarity over clutter, and smart defaults with full user control, this proposal outlines the design and implementation of SimpleChef as an HCI project.
